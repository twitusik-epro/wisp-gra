LONG DESCRIPTION (max 4000 characters):
Become Wisp — a glowing spirit of an enchanted forest — and guide it through 10 unique levels filled with challenges, enemies and secrets.

🌿 THE STORY
Wisp is the spirit of a sleeping forest that must reawaken the magic of its world. Each level brings a new environment, new enemies and new obstacles. Can you reach level 10?

🎮 GAMEPLAY
• Collect golden keys and activate exit portals
• Catch fireflies to earn points and climb the leaderboard
• Avoid enemies or jump on them from above — just like classic Mario
• Master the double jump to conquer harder platforms
• Fight dynamic wind that at higher levels can blow you right off the platform

🏆 WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT
• Completely ad-free — no banners, no video interruptions, ever
• Works without downloading large files — loads in seconds
• Progress sync between phone and computer (when logged in)
• Three difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard
• Global player leaderboard
• Badges and achievements system
• Procedural wind physics that affect your character — every run feels different
• Music and sound effects tuned to the forest atmosphere

📱 CONTROLS
• Virtual joystick (movement) + jump button (bottom right corner)
• Double jump — press twice to reach higher platforms

💚 FREE WITHOUT PRESSURE
Play completely free with no restrictions. Lives regenerate automatically every 30 minutes of active play. You can buy extra lives when you run out — but nobody forces you to.

Wisp — Spirit of the Forest is a game born from a passion for classic platformers and a love of nature. No corporate ad algorithms. Pure, peaceful gameplay.
