Delete Account & Data

Last updated: March 2026

How to request account deletion

If you wish to delete your Wisp account and all associated data, please send an email to:

contact@epro.pl

Use the subject line: Account deletion request — Wisp

Include the email address associated with your Google account used to sign in to Wisp.

⏱ We will process your request within 14 days and confirm deletion by email.

What data will be deleted

  • Your account (Google OAuth identifier)
  • Game progress (score, completed levels, badges)
  • Purchased lives and transaction history stored on our servers
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

What data will be retained

  • Anonymized, aggregated statistics (no personal identifiers)
  • Transaction records required by law (financial/tax obligations — up to 5 years)

Note about purchases

Deleting your account will permanently remove any unused lives or in-game items. These are non-refundable after account deletion. If you have a pending payment dispute, please resolve it before requesting deletion.

Alternative: Disconnect Google account

You can also revoke Wisp's access to your Google account without deleting your data. Go to Google Account Permissions, find Wisp, and remove access.
