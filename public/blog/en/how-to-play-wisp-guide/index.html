How to Play Wisp? Complete Beginner's Guide

March 15, 2026  ·  7 min read  ·  Wisp Editorial Team

Just started Wisp — Spirit of the Forest and not quite sure how the controls work, what to avoid, or how to push further into the later levels? You're in the right place. This guide covers everything from the absolute basics to the tricks that separate casual players from leaderboard climbers.

Who Is Wisp?

Wisp is the spirit of an enchanted forest — a glowing green creature whose world has gone quiet and dark. Its mission: travel through 40 levels and reawaken the magic that once made the forest alive. Each level brings new challenges, new enemies, and a new atmosphere. As you progress, the wind grows stronger, enemies move faster, and the physical demands on your reflexes become more intense — but so does the satisfaction of clearing each stage.

Controls

On Mobile (Touch)

A virtual joystick in the bottom-left corner handles left and right movement. The jump button in the bottom-right corner makes Wisp leap — press it again while airborne for a double jump.

On Desktop (Keyboard)

Use ← → or A/D to move left and right. Space, ↑, or W to jump. P or Escape to pause.

💡 Pro tip: The double jump is the most important technique in the game — press jump a second time while you're already in the air to reach higher platforms. You'll need this consistently from level 3 onwards. Practice it early.

Goals on Each Level

  1. Find the key — somewhere on the level lies a golden key. You cannot open the exit portal without it.
  2. Collect fireflies — each firefly is worth +100 points. They're not required to finish the level, but they're essential if you care about the leaderboard.
  3. Enter the portal — once you have the key, the exit portal activates. Step into it to advance to the next level.

Difficulty Levels

😊 Easy 5 lives, weaker wind, slower enemies
😐 Medium 3 lives, standard parameters
💀 Hard 1 life, strong wind, fast enemies

New players should start on Easy — it lets you learn the mechanics without frustration. Once you've cleared the first world on Easy, Medium will feel like a fresh challenge rather than a wall.

Enemies — How to Handle Them

You'll encounter two types of threats on your journey:

  • Patrolling enemies — they move back and forth along a set path. Study their rhythm and time your approach for when they're facing away.
  • Spike triangles — stationary traps. Side contact is lethal, but you can safely jump over them.

💡 Secret: You can defeat patrolling enemies by jumping directly on top of them — classic Mario style. You'll earn bonus points and clear the threat from the level entirely.

Wind — The Biggest Challenge on Higher Levels

Wisp is one of the few platform games where dynamic wind is a genuine gameplay mechanic, not just a visual effect. On higher levels, a strong gust can push you off a platform or carry you into spikes mid-jump if you're not accounting for it.

Here's how to manage it:

  • Check the wind direction before committing to any long jump
  • When jumping into the wind, aim slightly further than you normally would
  • On levels 8–10, the wind picks up — and in the later worlds — be ready to adjust your trajectory while airborne

⚠️ Warning: On Hard difficulty, wind is strong enough to throw Wisp completely off-screen. Stay toward the center of platforms and avoid lingering on edges.

Lives — What Happens When You Run Out

Every death — whether from an enemy, a fall into the void, or a spike — costs one life. When your lives are exhausted, you have a few options:

  • Log in and purchase extra lives — return to the game immediately
  • Start over — your best score remains on the leaderboard
  • Wait — every 30 minutes of active play earns you a free life

How to Earn Points and Climb the Leaderboard

  • Each firefly collected: +100 pts
  • Level completion: +500 pts + (level number × 50)
  • Defeating an enemy by jumping on it: +50 pts

The real path to the top is firefly collection. Players who collect every firefly across all 40 levels can hit scores above 15,000 points — that's what separates the leaderboard leaders from the rest of the field.

Badges — Hidden Challenges

Wisp includes an achievement badge system. Check them in the main menu. A few examples to aim for:

  • 🏆 Forest Veteran — complete the game (all 40 levels)
  • 💎 Firefly Collector — collect every firefly on a single level
  • ❤️ Supporter — purchase extra lives
